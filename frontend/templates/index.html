Welcome to Viscri

Step into a world where history meets beauty, where cobblestones whisper tales of generations past, and sunsets paint masterpieces across the sky. Nestled amidst rolling hills, our village invites you to explore its treasures and feel the warmth of its community. Immerse yourself in charming streets adorned with blooms, and indulge in local cuisine crafted with tradition. Join us in festivals showcasing our vibrancy and spirit. Whether seeking adventure or peace, Viscri welcomes you to discover magic around every corner. Come, be enchanted, and create memories in our timeless village.

Annual Flower Festival

Join us in celebrating the vibrant colors of spring at our Annual Flower Festival. Stroll through the picturesque village streets adorned with blooming flowers of all kinds, enjoy live music performances, and indulge in local delicacies.

Traditional Harvest Festival

Experience the essence of rural life at our Traditional Harvest Festival. Participate in age-old agricultural practices, such as harvesting crops and pressing grapes, and savor traditional dishes prepared with freshly harvested ingredients.

Village Fair and Market Day

Discover the charm of our local artisans and vendors at the Village Fair and Market Day. Browse through a variety of handmade crafts, artisanal goods, and locally grown produce while enjoying live entertainment and family-friendly activities.
