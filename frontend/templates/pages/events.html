Events and Experiences

Join us in Viscri for a variety of events celebrating our rich heritage and vibrant community. Check out our calendar for festivals, markets, tours, and more!

1. Annual Flower Festival

May 15th

Join us in celebrating the vibrant colors of spring at our Annual Flower Festival. Stroll through the picturesque village streets adorned with blooming flowers of all kinds, enjoy live music performances, and indulge in local delicacies.

2. Traditional Harvest Festival

September 20th

Experience the essence of rural life at our Traditional Harvest Festival. Participate in age-old agricultural practices, such as harvesting crops and pressing grapes, and savor traditional dishes prepared with freshly harvested ingredients.

3. Village Fair and Market Day

July 10th

Discover the charm of our local artisans and vendors at the Village Fair and Market Day. Browse through a variety of handmade crafts, artisanal goods, and locally grown produce while enjoying live entertainment and family-friendly activities.

4. Historical Walking Tour

Every Saturday

Step back in time and explore the rich history of our village with a guided Historical Walking Tour. Visit iconic landmarks, ancient ruins, and preserved heritage sites while learning fascinating stories about the village's past.

5. Seasonal Food Festival

October 5th - 7th

Indulge your taste buds in a culinary journey through the seasons at our Seasonal Food Festival. Sample an array of dishes featuring seasonal ingredients sourced from local farms and enjoy cooking demonstrations by renowned chefs.

6. Outdoor Concert Series

Every Friday night

Immerse yourself in the enchanting melodies of our Outdoor Concert Series. Set against the backdrop of stunning natural landscapes, these musical performances showcase a diverse range of genres and talents, sure to captivate audiences of all ages.

7. Community Volunteer Day

April 25th

Come together with fellow villagers and make a positive impact on our community during Community Volunteer Day. Engage in various volunteer activities, such as neighborhood cleanups, tree planting, and community garden maintenance, fostering a sense of unity and pride.

8. Artisan Workshop Series

Every Wednesday

Unleash your creativity and learn new skills at our Artisan Workshop Series. Led by talented local artisans, these hands-on workshops cover a wide range of traditional crafts, including pottery, weaving, woodworking, and more.

9. Nature Photography Expedition

June 8th

Embark on a journey of discovery and capture the breathtaking beauty of our village's natural landscapes on a Nature Photography Expedition. Guided by experienced photographers, explore hidden gems and scenic vistas while honing your photography skills.

10. Family Movie Night Under the Stars

August 30th

Gather your loved ones for a magical evening of cinema under the twinkling stars at our Family Movie Night. Bring blankets and picnic baskets to enjoy family-friendly films projected outdoors, creating cherished memories under the night sky.
