About Viscri

At the heart of our journey lies the quaint village of Viscri, a hidden gem nestled amidst the rolling hills and lush meadows of Transylvania. Here, history comes alive in the Fortified Church of Viscri, a UNESCO World Heritage Site dating back to the 12th century. Wander through its ancient walls and immerse yourself in the echoes of centuries past.

But Viscri is more than just its historic landmarks, it's a tapestry of tradition, culture, and warm hospitality. Explore the winding streets lined with traditional houses, each adorned with vibrant facades and intricate woodwork, and discover the timeless allure of rural life.

At Discover Viscri, we offer a range of services tailored to your every need. Whether you're craving adventure or seeking tranquility, our knowledgeable guides are here to curate the perfect experience for you. From guided tours through hidden corners of the village to traditional crafts workshops where you can immerse yourself in local artistry, we invite you to explore Viscri like never before.

And when it's time to unwind, let us be your trusted local partner. From assisting with accommodation bookings in cozy guesthouses to guiding you through the local handicrafts market, our dedicated team is here to ensure your stay in Viscri is nothing short of extraordinary.

Join us on an unforgettable journey of discovery and exploration as we invite you to experience the magic of Viscri, where history, culture, and natural beauty converge to create memories that last a lifetime.
